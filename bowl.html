Tips to improve your bowling skills

Focus on Your Run-Up:

Develop a consistent and smooth run-up. This sets the rhythm and balance needed for a controlled delivery.

Work on Your Grip:

Ensure you have a proper grip on the ball. For pace bowling, the seam should be upright between your index and middle fingers. For spin, adjust the grip according to the type of spin (off-spin or leg-spin).

Use Swing and Seam:

For pace bowlers, mastering swing (both in-swing and out-swing) and seam movement can make you more dangerous. Practice controlling the seam position.

Work on Fitness:

Good physical fitness helps with endurance and injury prevention, enabling you to maintain pace and accuracy throughout your spell.
