Tips to improve your feilding skills

Stay Low and Balanced:

Always stay in a low, balanced stance ready to move quickly in any direction. This helps in reacting faster to the ball.

Practice Catching Techniques:

Work on different catching techniques, including high catches, slip catches, and close-in catches. Soft hands and proper hand positioning are key.

Work on Diving and Sliding:

Practice diving and sliding techniques to stop boundaries or make crucial saves. Ensure your technique is safe to avoid injuries.

Improve Throwing Accuracy:

Practice throwing the ball accurately to the wicketkeeper or bowler. Focus on your aim, power, and technique to ensure your throws are both fast and precise.
