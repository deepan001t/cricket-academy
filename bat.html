Tips to improve your batting skills

Focus on Footwork:

Good footwork is crucial for playing both defensive and attacking shots. Practice moving forward, backward, and sideways with precision.

Watch the Ball:

Keep your eyes on the ball from the bowlerâ€™s hand until it hits the bat. This helps you judge the length, line, and pace better.

Master the Basic Shots:

Work on perfecting basic shots like the straight drive, cover drive, pull shot, and cut shot. These are essential for building an innings.

Practice Against Different Bowling Styles:

Train against fast bowlers, spinners, and medium pacers to become comfortable with all types of deliveries.
